## How I Think About Software

I like software that is simple to use, easy to evolve and honest about complexity. Good architecture matters, but so does the day-to-day experience of the people maintaining it and the people depending on it.

My work usually sits close to product needs: understanding the workflow, reducing friction, and turning messy requirements into tools that feel clear and reliable.
